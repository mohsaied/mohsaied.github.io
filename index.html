My research group is investigating how best to build the next generation of machine-learning-centric computer systems. We study methods to co-design and co-optimize neural networks and the hardware on which they run, with a special interest in reconfigurable computing. Our goal is to enable efficient deep learning in both mobile devices and datacenters. Our current research projects include:

  • End-to-end efficiency for large language Models (LLMs).
  • Automated machine learning (AutoML) and neural network compression.
  • New hardware architectures for machine learning.
  • Programmable hardware architectures based on FPGAs.
